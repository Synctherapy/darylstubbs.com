# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Health-conscious individuals, athletes, biohackers, and patients looking for credible, clinical-grade reviews of wellness technologies (red light therapy, hydrogen water, probiotics) without commercial bias.

## Product Purpose
To provide honest, peer-reviewed, hands-on reviews and protocol guides, helping readers make informed health investments based on clinical evidence and personal testing.

## Brand Personality
- **Clinical Authority**: Direct, precise, and grounded in research.
- **Editorial Restraint**: Understated print-like layout, avoiding loud or flashy components.
- **Evidence-First**: Clear visual emphasis on data, citations, and testing methodologies.

## Anti-references
- Generic affiliate marketing blogs featuring side-stripe borders, low-contrast text, excessive cards-inside-cards layouts, and marketing popups.
- SaaS template clichés such as big number metrics with gradient accents.
- Buzzwords like "supercharge", "unleash", "streamline", "seamless", or "next-generation".

## Design Principles
1. **Clinical Restraint**: Avoid heavy shadows, gradient text, and arbitrary rounded borders. The interface should feel clean and medical, yet warm.
2. **Typographic Integrity**: High contrast display and body text with optimal line widths (65–75ch) for readability.
3. **Evidence Visibility**: Proactively display clinical stat blocks and literature links.
4. **Physical Easing**: All transitions must feel snappy and responsive (under 200ms) with custom easing curves.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Contrast**: Maintain a strict contrast ratio of ≥4.5:1 for body and placeholder copy.
- **Reduced Motion**: Respect system preferences by removing transform-based motion for users with prefers-reduced-motion.
