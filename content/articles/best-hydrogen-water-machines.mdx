### What Is a Hydrogen Water Machine?

A hydrogen water machine is a device designed to infuse regular drinking water with molecular hydrogen (H₂) gas through a process called electrolysis. The result is hydrogen-rich water containing dissolved H₂ molecules, which are recognized as the smallest and most bioavailable selective antioxidants.

PPM (Parts Per Million) is the critical metric for measuring dissolved hydrogen concentration. Research studies show therapeutic effects at concentrations ranging from **0.5-1.6 PPM**. The premium machines I recommend can achieve up to **8 PPM**, far exceeding the established therapeutic threshold.

#### Types of Hydrogen Water Machines

*   **Portable Bottles:** Compact devices that generate hydrogen in small batches. Ideal for travel or on-the-go use. Example: Echo Flask.
*   **Pitchers:** Larger portable units designed for batch production. Excellent for families. Example: Echo Hydrogen Pitcher.
*   **Countertop Machines:** These connect directly to your faucet, providing on-demand hydrogen water. Example: Echo H2 Machine and Echo Ultimate.
