### Optimizing Your Experience: Clinical Administration

To maximize clinical outcomes, proper administration is critical. Here is the exact protocol I give my patients:

1.  **Use Room-Temperature Water:** The chemical reaction is most efficient in non-chilled water. Cold water slows the dissolution of the tablet.
2.  **Drop the Tablet In (Don't Stir):** Drop one tablet into 12-16 oz (350-475 ml) of water. Allow it to dissolve completely. Stirring causes the hydrogen gas to dissipate prematurely.
3.  **Drink Immediately:** Consume the water right away once the fizzing stops. Molecular hydrogen is a volatile gas that escapes into the atmosphere quickly.
4.  **Consider a Sealed Container:** Drinking from a bottle with a lid helps trap the H2 gas, increasing the concentration you consume.
