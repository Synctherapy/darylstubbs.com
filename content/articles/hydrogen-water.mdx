## What Is Hydrogen Water?

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube-nocookie.com/embed/BZW4slVdAIg" frameBorder="0" allowFullScreen></iframe>

Hydrogen-enriched water is simply H2O infused with dissolved molecular hydrogen gas (H2). It functions similarly to carbonated water, but uses H2 instead of CO2. 

Unlike broad-spectrum antioxidants that can interfere with necessary cellular signaling, H2 specifically targets damaging free radicals. This precision maintains cellular balance. In my clinic, I explain that modern stressors overwhelm our natural defenses. Supplementing with molecular hydrogen bolsters resilience against oxidative damage.

You can produce it using hydrogen-generating tablets, portable electrolysis bottles, or dedicated countertop machines. Many of my patients report that hydrogen water has a smoother, lighter taste than regular tap water, which naturally encourages better daily hydration.

## Understanding Composition and Production

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube-nocookie.com/embed/4tLF9abYa4I" frameBorder="0" allowFullScreen></iframe>

The most effective method for creating hydrogen-rich water is electrolysis. Devices pass water over platinum-coated titanium electrodes to split H2O into hydrogen and oxygen gas. 

High-quality devices use a Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) or Solid Polymer Electrolyte (SPE). This membrane separates H2 gas from O2 and chlorine, ensuring pure infusion. I strictly advise patients to avoid devices without SPE/PEM technology to prevent ingesting unwanted contaminants. 

Do not confuse hydrogen water with alkaline water. While older ionizers change the pH of the water, hydrogen water's clinical benefit comes entirely from the dissolved H2 gas.
