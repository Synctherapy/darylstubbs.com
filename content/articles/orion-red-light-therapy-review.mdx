## Incorporating Orion Into Your Daily Routine

Consistency dictates clinical outcomes. I instruct my patients to use their Orion device daily for 10 to 15 minutes per treatment area. Cells have a saturation point, meaning longer sessions do not yield better results. 

For skin protocols, position yourself 6 to 12 inches from the panel. Ensure the skin is clean and free of clothing or SPF. Hydration is critical; drink water before and after your session to facilitate cellular metabolism.

For deep tissue recovery, utilize the near-infrared setting. I recommend using the device in the morning to support natural circadian rhythms. Avoid heavy near-infrared exposure right before bed, as it can be mildly stimulating for some patients.

Always wear the provided eye protection when using the device on your face. If you are taking photosensitizing medications, consult your primary care physician before starting a photobiomodulation protocol. Ensure you are using your red light therapy devices exactly as directed to avoid mild skin irritation from overexposure.
