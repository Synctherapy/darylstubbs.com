## The 8 Wavelength Advantage: What's in the Light?

The proprietary 8-wavelength array separates the G4 series from standard dual-wavelength panels. Here is the clinical breakdown of tissue targeting.

### Red Light Wavelengths (630-670nm)

These wavelengths penetrate the skin's surface layers.

*   **630nm:** Stimulates cellular turnover and collagen synthesis in the upper dermis.
*   **650nm:** Accelerates superficial wound healing and localized microcirculation.
*   **660nm:** The primary wavelength for reducing skin inflammation and improving elasticity.
*   **670nm:** Drives ATP production in skin cells for rapid regeneration.

### Near-Infrared Wavelengths (810-1060nm)

These deeper-penetrating wavelengths reach muscles, joints, and neural tissue.

*   **810nm:** Penetrates deep into muscle tissue to clear inflammatory cytokines.
*   **830nm:** Accelerates mitochondrial respiration for rapid delayed-onset muscle soreness (DOMS) recovery.
*   **850nm:** Highly effective for reducing joint pain and improving mobility in osteoarthritic patients.
*   **1060nm:** The deepest penetrating wavelength, reaching bone, cartilage, and dense connective tissue.
