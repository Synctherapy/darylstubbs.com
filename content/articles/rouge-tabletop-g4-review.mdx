### Performance: Treatment Distance & Coverage

Clinical outcomes depend entirely on proper dosing and distance. The Tabletop G4 performs exceptionally well within its targeted scope.

**Optimal Treatment Distance:**
*   **6 inches (15cm):** This delivers maximum irradiance (~59 mW/cm²). I use this distance for deep tissue penetration, targeting specific joints, and stimulating mitochondrial activity. Patients report a distinct, soothing warmth.
*   **12 inches (30cm):** The beam spreads perfectly for full facial coverage. I prescribe this distance for dermatological protocols and superficial inflammation.
*   **18+ inches (45cm+):** Intensity drops significantly here. I only recommend this distance for highly sensitive areas or general maintenance.

**Coverage Area:**
This is a targeted device. It excels at treating specific anatomical regions:
*   **Face:** At 12 inches, it provides full facial coverage for anti-aging protocols and improving skin tone.
*   **Knee/Shoulder:** It comfortably covers an entire joint capsule, which is invaluable for arthritis or tendonitis recovery.
*   **Lower Back:** It effectively targets specific lumbar segments for localized pain relief.
*   **Full Body:** This device is not built for full-body systemic treatments. You need a larger panel like the Rouge Pro G4 or Max G4 for that.

**Treatment Time:**
For joint work, I prescribe 10-15 minute sessions at 6 inches. Facial protocols require 15-20 minutes at 12 inches. Consistency is the primary driver of cellular repair.
