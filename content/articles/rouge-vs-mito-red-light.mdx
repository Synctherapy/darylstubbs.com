### The Underlying Mechanism: Why Red Light Therapy Works

When evaluating this device category, we have to look at cellular respiration. Both Rouge and Mito panels emit photons that penetrate the skin and stimulate cytochrome c oxidase inside your mitochondria. 

This interaction upregulates the production of ATP (adenosine triphosphate). When your cells have more ATP, they repair tissue faster, modulate nitric oxide release, and clear oxidative stress. Patients often tell me they feel a noticeable reduction in delayed onset muscle soreness (DOMS) after just a few sessions. 

The visible red spectrum (630-700nm) handles superficial tissue repair, collagen synthesis, and skin health. The near-infrared (NIR) spectrum (800-1000nm+) handles the heavy lifting for clinical rehabilitation. These longer wavelengths bypass the skin to target muscle fascia, joint capsules, and bone tissue.
