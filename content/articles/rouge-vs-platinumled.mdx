## Key Differences: A Clinician's Perspective

### **Power & Coverage**
The PlatinumLED BioMax 900 features a wider 36" × 12" frame designed for comprehensive, full-body sessions. This broader coverage stimulates more mitochondria across a larger surface area, driving systemic benefits. I use this setup for athletes dealing with widespread inflammatory conditions. The Rouge Pro G4 is slightly narrower, making it ideal for targeted half-body treatments or specific joint rehabilitation.

### **Price Gap**
The Rouge Pro G4 costs roughly $875 CAD, while the PlatinumLED BioMax 900 starts around $1,299 USD. PlatinumLED requires nearly double the investment when factoring in currency conversion and import duties for Canadian patients.
