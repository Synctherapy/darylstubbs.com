### The Science of Photobiomodulation

Photobiomodulation (PBM) works by delivering specific wavelengths of red and near-infrared (NIR) light directly to your cells. These photons are absorbed by cytochrome c oxidase within the mitochondria, leading to:
*   **Elevated ATP Production:** Increases cellular energy to accelerate tissue repair.
*   **Nitric Oxide Synthesis:** Enhances microcirculation, accelerating nutrient delivery to recovery zones.
*   **Reduced Cellular Stress:** Dampens systemic inflammatory pathways.

In my clinical practice, these mechanisms translate to accelerated injury rehabilitation, reduced chronic pain, and improved skin elasticity.
