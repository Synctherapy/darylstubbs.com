### Key Differentiators of the G4 Series

*   **8 Wavelength Spectrum:** Each panel delivers a unique blend of red (630nm, 650nm, 660nm, 670nm) and NIR (810nm, 830nm, 850nm, 1060nm) wavelengths for comprehensive cellular stimulation.
*   **Multichip LED Technology:** Every single LED bulb contains all 8 wavelengths, ensuring uniform power delivery and preventing cold spots.
*   **Custom Dimming Controls:** Users can independently adjust the brightness of each of the 8 wavelengths, allowing for highly customized therapies.
*   **Independent Pulsing:** The G4 series enables pulsing at frequencies ranging from 1 to 10,000Hz, which may enhance mitochondrial response.

### Understanding Wavelength Depths

Red wavelengths (630nm to 670nm) target dermal tissues. They support collagen synthesis and skin healing. NIR wavelengths (810nm to 1060nm) reach deeper muscle layers and joints, supporting recovery and relieving stiffness.
